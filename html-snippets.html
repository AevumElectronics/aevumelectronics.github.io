Survey Form Example

Survey form where name and email are required.

Age is optional and restricted between min/max numbers

The first fieldset is a dropdown menu made with "select"

The second and third fieldsets are a series of input in the form of "radio" and "checkbox"

The last fieldset is a "textarea" input and lastly there is a submit button to send all the data.
