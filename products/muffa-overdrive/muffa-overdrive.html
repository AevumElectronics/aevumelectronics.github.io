Eurorack Muffa Overdrive

A Vintage Overdrive Made With Modern Components


Muffa is a vintage overdrive made with modern components.
The main goal of this project is to create and build a guitar effect that belongs to the eurorack ecosystem.
The idea is to build a module as simple as possible with the characteristics of a vintage stompbox without the reliability issue of old electronics, capable of synth level volumes even for small inputs.
With an output that can swing between ±5Vpp we can achieve an unprecedented headroom and maintain a low noise environment even with a gain that goes near 70dB.

Analog Heart


The front panel has two 3.5mm audio jacks for the signal input and output, a bypass switch, and 3 knobs for the control.
The selection of the components is paramount so, while maintaining an all true analog approach, we look at the future with high quality and low noise components.
The control panel is as simple as intuitive:
The volume knob lets you select the right amount of output, the sustain/gain knob goes from almost clean and rounded to a heavy and fuzzy sound, with a led distortion and a huge amount of gain.
Finally, the tone knob, built with a vintage mindset with a typical scooped midrange, let you sweep from a muffled low-pass to an acid high-pass with a wide variety of sweet middle characters.

Specs:

  • 10 Pin Power Connector
  • Dimension: 3U 4HP
  • SMD components
  • Skiff Friendly
  • Led Distortion
  • Synth Level Volume ±5Vpp
  • Gain: 60dB
